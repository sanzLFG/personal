Personal Tracker — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Personal Tracker ("the App") is a private, single-user, non-commercial application operated by an individual for their own personal use. The App exists solely to let its operator collect, view, and analyze their own health and fitness data. It has no other users, offers no service to the public, and is not operated by a business.

1. Who this policy covers

The only person whose data the App accesses is the App's own operator, who is also the only user. The operator connects their own accounts and views only their own data.

2. What data the App accesses

When the operator connects their WHOOP account, the App accesses that account's data through the official WHOOP API, with the operator's explicit consent granted at connection time. This may include recovery metrics, sleep data, daily cycle and strain data, workout data, and basic profile information. The App does not access any data belonging to anyone other than its operator.

3. How the data is used

Data is used exclusively for the operator's personal purposes: displaying trends over time, and generating personal fitness, sleep, and lifestyle insights for the operator's own reference. The data is never used for advertising, profiling of others, or any commercial purpose.

4. How the data is stored and secured

Data is stored in a private database controlled solely by the operator. Data is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) and is protected at rest by the hosting provider. Access credentials are kept in secure, non-public storage and are not exposed publicly.

5. Data sharing

The App does not sell, rent, share, or transfer the operator's data to any third party. No data leaves the operator's own private storage except as needed to display it back to the operator.

6. Data retention and deletion

Data is retained only as long as the operator wishes to keep it. The operator can delete any or all stored data at any time, and can revoke the App's access to their WHOOP account at any time through their WHOOP account settings. Deleting the App's access does not affect the operator's underlying WHOOP account or data held by WHOOP.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the App's functionality changes. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.

This is the privacy policy for a personal, single-user application. It is not a commercial service and collects no data from any person other than its operator.
